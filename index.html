About

Learn how Gameya came along

  • 2005

    Humble Beginnings

    When the founder used to play shop with her grandma, she named it "Gameya". Ever since, she knew she was going to have her own business one day and name it Gameya.

  • 2015

    Passion Found

    Founder took her first computer science class in high school and fell in love. It fulfilled her creative and logical mind. Throughout high school and college, she pursued her passion and gained technical skills to build her first website.

  • 2023

    Gameya Founded

    Gameya was officially founded in 2023 fulfilling her childhood dream. Gameya helps individuals and small business owners to grow their online presence and ultimately pursue their passion!
